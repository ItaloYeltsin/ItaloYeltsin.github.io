About me

I am taking Master's Degree and have a Bachelor in Computer Science at the State University of Ceará (UECE) . Also, I am a member of the Search-Based Software Engineering Group (GOES.UECE) since 2012, which is headed by Professor Jerffeson Teixeira de Souza. My research interests are concentrated on Mathematical Optimisation, having focused on Search-Based Software Engineering and worked with Genetic Algorithms and Interactive optimization applied to software engineering problems. My current research involves the use of a mathematical model based on scalarization function to control de relevance of software metrics during the optimisation process in Software Engineering optimization problems.
