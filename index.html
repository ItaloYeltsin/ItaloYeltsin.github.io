About me

I am Master in Computer Science from the State University of Ceará (UECE) and Bachelor in Information Systems from the Faculdade de Juazeiro do Norte (FJN). Currently, I am professor in the courses of Information Systems and Computer Science at Federal University of Ceará (UFC) - Campus Crateús, doctorate student in Management at State University of Ceará (UECE) and member of Optimization in Software Engineering Group of UECE (GOES.UECE). I was member of the 6th International Symposium on Search Based Software Engineering (SSBSE´14) organizing committee. Overall, my field of study covers the Information Systems and addresses the development of support decision models to managerial issues, especially in Financial Technology and Software Engineering areas. My research interests are focused to the following subjects: Asset Management, Management Science and Search Based Software Engineering.
